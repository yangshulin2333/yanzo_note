# Market Intelligence

Actionable market intelligence from Jabil’s supply chain and procurement experts at your fingertips.

##### Market intelligence gives you the insights you need to make smart, strategic sourcing and design decisions with confidence. By digging into data on market trends, supplier performance, component availability, and the competition, companies can spot shifts early, manage  risks, and tweak strategies to stay ahead. With the right intel, navigating an ever-changing market becomes a whole lot easier.

At Jabil, we take market intelligence beyond the basics. Our industry experts gather real-world data and deliver actionable insights to help you navigate the complexities of today’s supply chain landscape. Whether you’re dealing with fluctuating prices or potential supplier delays, we provide the intelligence you need to make confident, strategic decisions.

## **How Jabil’s Market Intelligence Drives Informed Sourcing Decisions**

##### Jabil has earned a trusted reputation for optimizing manufacturing and supply chain networks for over 400 of the world’s leading brands. Our hands-on analysis blends qualitative assessments and quantitative data to deliver actionable insights that go beyond traditional research.

With a vast network of over 38,000 suppliers and more than 700,000 managed parts across various market segments, Jabil gathers billions of data points daily. This includes:

- **Commodity Availability:**  Track real-time supply and demand of [electronic](https://pscs.jabil.com/market-intelligence/commodity-intelligence.html) and [mechanical](https://pscs.jabil.com/market-intelligence/mechanical-intelligence.html) commodities to anticipate shortages and adjust plans accordingly.
- **Pricing Trends:** Use predictive pricing data to secure better rates and avoid unexpected costs.
- **Supplier Lead Times:** Access predicted lead times to prevent delays and keep operations running smoothly.
- **Category Trends:** Plan ahead for energy, MRO, and labor trends.
- **Logistics Conditions:** Get insights on shipping and transportation to streamline deliveries and avoid disruptions.

Jabil provides intelligence on eight of the most in-demand end markets. Our experts deliver in-depth analysis of market trends, technological advancements, and supply chain risks and opportunities. These insights come with tailored recommendations, helping businesses navigate economic challenges and mitigate risks specific to their industries.

##### Market intelligence unlocks a wealth of data that most companies simply can’t access on their own. At Jabil, we go beyond the surface, combining extensive data points from competitors, industry trends, customer demand, and more to deliver a comprehensive view of your business landscape. 

During periods of economic uncertainty, Jabil’s market intelligence services have allowed our partners to identify alternative suppliers, secure critical components, and streamline procurement — all while maintaining cost efficiency and operational agility.
