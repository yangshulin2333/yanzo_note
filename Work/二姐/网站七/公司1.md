# We are Pionix. Powering Future Movement.

As initiators and major contributors of the open-source EVerest project, we combine engineering expertise with proven solutions to help manufacturers accelerate development and certification. Our mission: make EV charging reliable, interoperable, and future-ready – for everyone.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/67ed4427fd390f3007c5a1f7/6808b5f29774daef9d92655b_About%20Header%20Image%20\(1\).avif)

## Our commitment

Our commitment to collaboration nurtures a vibrant community of industry leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts, all working together to overcome challenges and shape the future of mobility for generations to come.

### Open source innovation

We actively contribute to EVerest, the open-source operating system for EV chargers. By aligning innovation with community-driven development, we accelerate interoperability and make EV charging more accessible.

### Collaborative community

We believe in working hand in hand – with manufacturers, developers, and ecosystem partners. This collaboration fuels faster progress, better solutions, and a sustainable charging infrastructure.

### Reliable, future-ready solutions

We provide engineering services and validated components that enable manufacturers to integrate EVerest more quickly, reduce certification risks, and bring robust products to market.

## How it started.  
How it’s going.

2025 • Today

### Stronger focus on open source.

We have started the process of donating BaseCamp assets into the open-source EVerest project under LF Energy. This marks a new chapter for Pionix, doubling down on our commitment to open collaboration, interoperability, and the global EVerest community.

2025 • March

### We join up with PHYTEC to create and deploy the EVCS-Cube.

The EVCS-Cube is a comprehensive development kit for AC/DC charging solutions that combines PHYTEC’s phyVERSO-EVCS charger controller with our BaseCamp operating system.

2025 • January

### We officially release Pionix BaseCamp.

After years of development, we finally officially release our powerful, open source operating system for EV charging solutions. Based on the EVerest framework, BaseCamp is designed to streamline product development and ensure compatibility with the latest industry standards.

2024 • October

### Pionix grows to 40 team members.

We celebrate a major milestone as our team has expanded to 40 talented members. As we continue to grow, we keep fighting together to revolutionize the EV charging industry.

2024 • February

### We host the "Open Source in Emobility" conference and the OCA Plugfest.

With 150 participants from 17 countries, our "Open Source in Emobility" conference proves a huge success. The OCA Plugfest follows suit, as developers of charging stations and charging station management systems from all over the world meet at Pionix.

2023 • April

### We secure over €5 million in seed funding.

We successfully raise over €5 million in an oversubscribed Seed financing round, led by yabeo Impact AG and Pale blue dot, to advance our open source EVerest stack for more standardized and efficient development in the EV charging industry.

2022 • January

### EVerest Project becomes an official project of the LFE.

We are proud that EVerest is now an official project of the Linux Foundation Energy and driven by a global community of industry players, standardization bodies, government agencies, research institutes and EV charging enthusiasts.

2021 • February

### Pionix is founded.

We firmly believe that open source technology will play a pivotal role in the future of EV charging. This is why we founded Pionix and initiated the EVerest project.
