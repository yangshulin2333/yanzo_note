# The Cyber Resilience Act

How the EV Charging Industry Needs to Act Now

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/67ed4427fd390f3007c5a1f7/68aefde4221ed28686959b27_white-paper-cyber-resilience.avif)

The introduction of the Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) marks a pivotal moment for the EV charging industry, urging enterprises to elevate their cybersecurity measures and ensure compliance with **new regulations**.This imperative legislation is designed to bolster defenses against cyber threats while safeguarding the integrity and reliability of critical infrastructure.

In our comprehensive white paper, Pionix experts

- dissect the implications of the CRA
- map out clear strategies that enterprises can adopt to **ensure compliance**
- show ways to protect systems from emerging threats
