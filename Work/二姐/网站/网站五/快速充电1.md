## Fast charging solutions tailored for every need

Revolutionising EV Charging in the UK

Revolutionising EV Charging in the UK

Wallbox has partnered with Osprey Charging Network, one of the UK’s largest and most trusted rapid EV charging networks, to improve charge point availability and energy efficiency.

- The Challenge: Providing a reliable charging experience in low-voltage connection stations
- The Solution: Supernova software platform seamlessly integrated with Osprey’s Iris platform via OCPP
- The Outcome: +50 Supernovas installed across UK, and counting. On average, each Supernova will save an impressive 60.8 tons of CO2 over its lifecycle

[Learn More](https://wallbox.com/en_uk/blog/wallbox-collaborates-with-osprey-charging-network-to-enhance-ev-charging-access-and-efficiency-in-the-uk)

Powering Up Madrid

Powering Up Madrid

With a shared commitment to advancing EV adoption and reducing carbon emissions, this collaboration has set the stage for transformative projects, including the latest milestone in Madrid’s city center.

- The Challenge: meet high standards and new regulatory requirements
- The Solution: Supernova scalability, ideal for indoor parking that need modular solutions to meet evolving needs
- The Outcome: Biggest hub in Madrid, with the capacity to recharge up to 300 EV daily

[Learn More](https://wallbox.com/en/blog/eranovum-wallbox-fast-ev-charging-madrid)

Deploying fast-charging in Milan

Deploying fast-charging in Milan

CityLife, a prominent urban regeneration project in Europe, aimed to integrate fast-charging infrastructure into its sustainability strategy.

- The Challenge: Install EV chargers in an area visited by over 10 million people annually
- The Solution: Supernova compact design and footprint offering unmatched installation flexibility
- The Outcome: EV charging successfully integrated in the Milan City Life project

[Learn More](https://wallbox.com/en/blog/wallbox-atlantes-sustainable-fast-charging-initiative)

Bringing fast charging to Retail

Bringing fast charging to Retail

Colruyt Group, Belgium’s largest food retailer, is spearheading an ambitious project to equip its stores with EV chargers.

- The Challenge: provide charging solution that ensures maximum uptime and supports the company’s revenue-generating strategy of selling green energy
- The Solution: Supernova’s high-power output enable customers to add up to 300 km of range to their EVs during a typical 30 mins shopping trip
- The Outcome: Each charging location annually reduces consumption by 30,000 liters of conventional fuels, slashing CO2 emissions by 73,000 kg

[Learn More](https://wallbox.com/en/blog/ev-charging-sustainable-initiative)

##### Energy Management

### Sirius Energy Intelligence (SEI)

Designed for more complex systems, SEI reduces energy costs by ensuring that your site is using all of its available power efficiently.

SEI allows for dynamic load balancing across DC chargers, DC and AC chargers combinations, or multiple power circuits.

It's future-ready for seamless integration with solar PV systems, batteries, and fleets of bidirectional EVs.
