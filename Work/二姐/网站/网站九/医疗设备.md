# Medical Devices

![medical-devices-header](https://jabil.com/dam/jcr:fc881939-b8ba-4318-9b61-dd2f60e6e8fe/medical-technology-header.jpg)

Jabil provides advanced manufacturing solutions across a wide range of medical devices, surgical instruments, capital equipment, patient monitoring equipment, chronic disease management, digital healthcare tools, and more.

Exceptional engineering and design capabilities — combined with our unparalleled vertically integrated manufacturing competencies — makes Jabil the trusted choice to empower innovation and help deliver mechanically intricate, technology-rich medical products to market.

## Macro Trends Transforming Care

Medical technology and digital health innovations are reshaping the delivery of care, offering improved care management, and more precise, and personally tailored treatments.

Medical Device Technology Capabilities

Jabil’s strategic investments in specialized capabilities provide our customers turnkey solutions at lower cost with a range of medical application, including:

- Electrical component integration/on-board electronics  
     
- Advanced optical module assembly  
     
- Active alignment  
     
- Sensor integration  
     
- Complex catheter subassemblies and finished products  
     
    - Guide, diagnostic, balloon  
         
- Disposable endoscopes  
     
- Robotic assisted surgical systems  
     
    - Arm manipulator end-effector

- Minimally invasive surgical instruments (trocars/forceps)  
     
- Vision systems; power units; RF ablation; smoke evacuators  
     
- Microwelding/ultrasonic welding  
     
- Micro molding  
     
- Micro assembly  
     
- Soldering  
     
- Electrical testing  
     
- Complex assembly and calibration  
     
- Sterilization
