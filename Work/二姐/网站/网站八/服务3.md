## Advanced manufacturing capabilities to help you build innovative, high-quality products faster and at scale

We continually enhance our advanced manufacturing capabilities by investing in a focused [portfolio of technologies](https://flex.com/solutions-and-services/manufacturing#industry-4-0), including simulation, automation and robotics, digitization, and additive manufacturing.

Along with our trusted [supply chain network](https://flex.com/solutions-and-services/supply-chain), these capabilities fuel our flexible, global manufacturing and increase efficiency and quality. We optimize our flexible manufacturing models to meet your time-to-market, resiliency, and end-market requirements.

We leverage our supply chain network, domain expertise, scale, and talented employees to fulfill your manufacturing requirements, regardless of complexity, region, or market.

And we deliver maximum value with disciplined execution to ensure the highest quality and efficiency. Together, we can create products that improve the world.
