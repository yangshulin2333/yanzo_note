Used to provide power from a DC source to an application of a different DC voltage or to stabilize existing voltages.

    - **Configuration:** Isolated versions available.
        
    - **Control:** Remote on/off connector included.
        
    - **Functionality:** Protects alternators in lithium systems by preventing overload and increases battery life through sophisticated charging features.
        
- **Models Mentioned:** Orion XS, Orion-Tr, Orion.

## PART 10: THE DEEP DIVE (Technical/Content Layer)

### Category: MPPT Sizing Calculator

- **Core Function**: Provides technical validation for solar charge controller configurations based on specific environmental and hardware variables.
    
- **Input Variables**:
    
    - **Solar Panels**: Selection of specific modules (e.g., Victron Energy 175W-12V Mono) with series and string counts.
        
    - **Environment**: Temperature extremes (Min/Max) to calculate voltage shifts.
        
    - **Cabling**: Accounting for cable length and cross-section to determine voltage drop.
        
    - **System Voltage**: Battery bank voltage (e.g., 12V) and geographic location (e.g., Almere, Netherlands).
