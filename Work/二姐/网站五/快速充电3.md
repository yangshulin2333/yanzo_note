##### Our Ecosystem

### End-to-end charging and storage, managed through a single point of contact.

#### Supernova Fast Charging

Delivers up to 240 kW. Compatible with low and high-voltage EVs.

#### Pramac’s BESS

Scalable battery storage up to 2.1 MWh. Ideal for urban, remote, and industrial sites.

#### Energy Management System (EMS)

Seamless interconnectivity & load management. Ensures optimized energy flow between Supernova and Pramac’s BESS.

#### After-sales & Support

A single point of contact for maintenance, monitoring & upgrades.
