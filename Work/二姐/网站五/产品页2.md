##### Energy Management

# Your charging system is now an energy management solution.

With more features than any other charger, Quasar 2 will redefine energy use at home. Unlock its full potential with a Wallbox power meter and our built-in energy management features.

Vehicle-to-Home

Vehicle-to-Home

Power your home with the energy stored in your EV. Whether you're storing energy from your solar panels or the grid, you'll save big during peak hours.

- Power for up to 3 days
- Less expensive than battery storage
- Avoid peak energy costs

Vehicle-to-Grid

Vehicle-to-Grid

Send energy back to the grid during peak hours to earn incentives from your utility company and support widespread EV adoption by helping to stabilize the grid.

- Earn incentives
- Support widespread EV adoption
- Generate and share clean energy

Solar Charging

Solar Charging

Harness the full power of your solar panels. Avoid high grid energy prices when you charge your EV with 100% solar energy or a mix of solar and grid energy. The choice is yours.

- Customizable settings
- Sustainable
- Maximized ROI on solar panel installation

Peak Support

Peak Support

Automatically pull energy from your EV to top up your home’s energy allotment during peak consumption to avoid billing surprises. With safety discharge, there is no power limit. Charge on.

- No surprise energy bills
- Automatic
- Protects your electrical system
