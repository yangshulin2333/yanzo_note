# Services that maximize uptime and support your business

## Get peace of mind with Wallbox. Our comprehensive services offerings, including training, troubleshooting, maintenance, and after-sales support, ensure you're always supported.

##### Wallbox Care

### Customizable services for your business

Proactive support keeps your Supernova running. Our tailored services cover everything from remote diagnostics and on-site repairs to parts management, maintenance, and warranty extensions.

#### Remote Support

Our Customer Care Center will assist you remotely, resolve issues with your chargers and guide you.

#### Corrective & Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance visits will be conducted to optimize performance and minimize downtime. If an issue arises, a Wallbox Certified Agent will be onsite promptly to ensure your charger is back in operation quickly.

#### Warranty Extension

Extend your coverage beyond the standard Wallbox warranty timeframe, providing protection against the unexpected and peace of mind that your assets are covered.

#### Spare Parts

In order to extend your charger's lifespan we offer individual components that can be used to replace damaged or worn-out components.
