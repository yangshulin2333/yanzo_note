### Comparison: Lithium-ion vs. Lead-acid Batteries

|**Parameter**|**Lithium-ion Battery**|**Lead-acid Battery**|
|---|---|---|
|**Maintenance**|Maintenance-free, with BMS|Regular watering, acid refilling, equalizing charge|
|**Initial Cost**|Higher (continuously decreasing)|Lower|
|**5-Year TCO**|Lower|Higher|
|**Operating Cost (2.5T)**|¥1253/month|¥2380/month|
|**Lifespan (Cycles)**|3000–5000 cycles|800–1200 cycles|
|**Warranty Period**|5 years|2 years|
|**Charging Time**|2–3 hours|6–10 hours|
|**Opportunity Charge**|Supported (no memory effect)|Not recommended (shortens lifespan)|
|**Energy Efficiency**|> 92%|~ 60%|
|**Replacement**|Not required|Usually two batteries per forklift|
|**Temp Tolerance**|Excellent (Thermal Management)|Poor in extreme temperatures|
|**Safety**|Safer (BMS, no leakage)|Risk of spills/gas emissions|
|**Infrastructure**|Requires smart charger|Dedicated, ventilated area|
