Choosing between **Lithium-ion** and **Lead-acid forklift batteries** is one of the most important decisions for warehouse and industrial fleet operators. Both battery types offer specific benefits based on your application, operating hours, and budget.

This forklift battery comparison datasheet highlights key differences in charging speed, maintenance, energy efficiency, and lifecycle costs to help you select the best power solution for your electric forklifts. It’s an essential guide for improving warehouse productivity and reducing long-term operating expenses.

  

  

|   |   |   |
|---|---|---|
||Lithium-ion Battery|Lead-acid Battery|

### Parameter

|   |   |   |
|---|---|---|
|Maintenance|Maintenance-free, equipped with BMS (Battery Management System)|Requires regular watering, acid refilling, and equalizing charge|
|Initial Cost|Higher (but costs are continuously decreasing)|Lower|
|5-Year Total Cost of Ownership|Lower (long lifespan, high efficiency, no maintenance)|Higher (frequent replacement, energy and labor costs)|
|2.5T Forklift Operating Cost (Normal workload, electricity ¥1/kWh, 8h/day, 30 days/month)|¥1253/month|¥2380/month|
|Lifespan (Charge Cycles)|3000–5000 cycles|800–1200 cycles|
|Warranty Period|5 years|2 years|
|Charging Time|2–3 hours (supports opportunity charging)|6–10 hours|
|Opportunity Charging|Supported – ideal for frequent short charging (no memory effect)|Not recommended – frequent charging shortens lifespan|
|Energy Efficiency|Greater than 92%|Around 60%|
|Battery Replacement Required|Not required|Required – usually need two batteries per forklift|
|Temperature Tolerance|Excellent – with built-in thermal management system|Poor performance in extreme temperatures|
|Safety|Safer – BMS protection, no acid or gas leakage|Risk of acid spills and hydrogen gas emissions|
|Weight|Lighter – improves forklift efficiency|Heavier – adds to vehicle load|
|Charging Infrastructure|Requires smart charger|Requires a dedicated, ventilated charging area|
|Charging Interface|DC charging port / EMA plug|EMA plug / Anderson|
|Environmental Impact|Environmentally friendly, sealed design, recyclable|Contains harmful substances, requires special disposal|
|Equipment Availability / Uptime|Very high – minimal downtime, fast charging|Lower – slow charging and frequent battery swaps needed|
|Typical Applications|Multi-shift operations, intensive logistics, cold-chain warehousing, etc.|Single-shift, low-frequency use, small warehouses, etc.|
