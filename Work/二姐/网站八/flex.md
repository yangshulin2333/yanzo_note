## PART 3: POWER AND COOLING PRODUCTS (Technical Deep Dive)

### Critical Power Products

Our products and services from **Anord Mardix** and **Crown Technical Systems** deliver safe, reliable, and high-quality critical power solutions.

#### Busway

- **Databar**: Available in UL and IEC; independently certified; two busway designs.
    
- **IBar**: Designed for flexibility; vertical and horizontal power distribution; busbar ratings from 800A to 6300A.
    
- **Resinbar**: Independently certified; modular installation; minimal waste produced compared to cable.
    

#### Switchgear

- **Distribution Panelboards**: Molded case circuit breakers (MCCB) and MCCB switch disconnectors up to 800A; ASTA tested 10-way TP&N and 4 Pole versions.
    
- **LV Switchgear**: Top, bottom, front, or rear cable access; smallest footprint (ASTA test) in the market; seismically tested and certified.
    
- **MV Switchgear**: Available from 11kV to 36kV; G59/G99 protection capability; pre-engineered and bespoke MV protection/control designs.
    
- **MV Metal Clad Switchgear**: Air or vacuum circuit breakers (4.16KV to 38KV); meets/exceeds ANSI/IEEE (ANSI C37.20.2) and NEMA standards.
    
- **Packaged Substations**: HV circuit breakers or MV switch disconnectors; air-cooled or forced ventilation transformer options.
    
- **Power Distribution Units**: Integrated static transfer switches available; certified to 61439-2 up to 50kA/1s; solutions up to 1250A.
    
- **Remote Power Panels**: Available for North America and EMEA; highly compact footprint.
    
- **Static Transfer Switch**: Modular STS; hot-swappable fans and power supplies; Listed to UL 60950 and UL 891.
    
- **UL891 Switchgear**: Tested and built to UL 891; seismically tested and certified to IBC and OSHPD.
    
- **UL1558 Switchgear**: Tested to ANSI C37.51; NEMA Type 1 enclosure; high short-time withstand up to 150kA for 1 second (60 cycles).
    

#### Modular Solutions and Enclosures

- **Power Control Assembly**: No size limit; customized and developed in cooperation with customers.
    
- **Power Distribution Center**: Flexible solutions; customized development.
    
- **Power Pods**: Fully fitted and factory tested in ISO 9001 environment; robust, weatherproof; minimum 25-year lifespan.
    
- **Power Skids**: Built to exact specifications in controlled factory environment; Level 3 tested.
    
- **Traction Power Supply Substation**: All equipment installed, cabled, and tested.
    
- **Relay Panels**: Full duplex, half duplex, and cabinets.
    

#### Power Management Systems

- **Energy Management Systems**: Automatic data acquisition from devices across sites; web-enabled real-time monitoring; alarms and events log.
    
- **ims DCIM**: Universal database interface; configurable system access; scalable modular system.
    
- **Modular Circuit Monitoring Systems**: Single monitoring solution; presence of voltage detection; predictive circuit health analysis.
    
- **SCADA Systems**: Dynamic, fully customizable user interface; high-speed processing of real-time data and alarms.
    

#### Field Services

- **Project Installation**: Staff ECS/SSSTS/SMSTS qualified; full traceability; operations across UK and EMEA.
    
- **Training Capabilities**: Bespoke training in controlled, safe environments.
    
- **Third-party Systems and Upgrades**: Testing of legacy equipment per BS7671 and BS6423:1983; comprehensive servicing.
    
- **Service and Maintenance**: Custom contracts; 24-hour target response time; includes all parts, consumables, and labor.

## PART 4: JETCOOL SMARTPLATE™ SYSTEM (Specific Product Layer)

### Overview and Mandate

- **System Type**: Chassis-level, closed-loop, liquid-assisted air cooling.
    
- **Core Technology**: Patented microconvective cooling® technology.
    
- **Target Infrastructure**: Deployable in existing air-cooled data centers without facility modifications.
    

### Performance Metrics

- **Density**: 3X denser compute than competing liquid cooling technologies.
    
- **Cost Efficiency**: 50% lower upfront costs than microchannel cold plates or immersion installations.
    
- **Rack Support**: Up to 50kW air-cooled racks.
    
- **Energy Savings**: Up to 4kW per rack reduction in energy consumption by reducing server-level fan power.
    

### Key Components

- **Enhanced Cold Plates**: Two units per system targeting processor hotspots.
    
- **Self-Contained Loop**: Includes a pump and radiator within the chassis.
    

### JetCool Product Portfolio

- **SmartPlate Cold Plate**: Fully-sealed cold plate leveraging microconvective cooling®.
    
- **SmartLid**: Fluid-to-die direct impingement cooling targeting processor hotspots.
    
- **SmartSilicon**: In-silicon cooling embedding directly into chip substrate for maximum temperature control.
