# Flex: Transforming for a new era of technology innovation

For over 55 years, we’ve been the manufacturing partner of choice for leading brands, enabling them to bring critical technologies to market. Our strategy is focused on enhancing our core manufacturing and supply chain capabilities while expanding our portfolio of proprietary products and value-added services, going above and beyond to simplify our customer’s product lifecycle development and production.

![Our Vision](https://flex.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/AboutUs_Our-Vision-Icon.svg)

## Our vision

Be the most trusted global technology, supply chain, and advanced manufacturing solutions partner.

![Our Mission](https://flex.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/AboutUs_Our-Mission-Icon.svg)

## Our mission

Leverage our expertise, capabilities, and [unmatched global reach](https://flex.com/company/global-locations) to create exceptional products that make a positive impact on the world.
