## Switchgear

### Distribution panelboards

Key features:

- Molded case circuit breakers (MCCB) and MCCB switch disconnectors up to 800A 
- ASTA tested 10 way TP&N and 4 Pole versions with ratings up to 800A

[View distribution panelboards](https://anordmardix.com/products/distribution-panelboards/)

### LV switchgear

Key features:

- Top, bottom, front, or rear cable access is possible 
- Smallest footprint (ASTA test) in the market 
- Seismically tested and certified

[View LV switchgear](https://anordmardix.com/products/lv-switchboards/)

### MV switchgear

Key features:

- Available from 11kV to 36kV 
- G59/G99 protection capability 
- Pre-engineered and bespoke MV protection and control scheme designs 

[View MV switchgear](https://anordmardix.com/products/mv-switchgear/)

### MV metal clad switchgear

Key features:

- Air or vacuum circuit breakers within the range of 4.16KV to 38KV 
- Meets or exceeds applicable ANSI/IEEE (ANSI C37.20.2) and NEMA standards 

[View MV metal clad switchgear](https://crowntechnicalsystems.com/products/switchgear)

### Packaged substations

Key features:

- HV circuit breakers or MV switch disconnectors 
- Top or bottom HV cable connections 
- Air cooled or forced ventilation Transformer options

[View packaged substations](https://anordmardix.com/products/packaged-substations/)

### Power distribution units

Key features:

- Integrated static transfer switches available 
- Certified to 61439-2 up to 50kA/1s with solutions available up to 1250A

[View power distribution units](https://anordmardix.com/products/power-distribution-unit/)

### Remote power panels

Key features:

- Available for North America and EMEA
- Highly compact footprint

[View remote power panels](https://anordmardix.com/products/remote-power-panel/)

### Static transfer switch

Key features:

- Modular STS 
- Hot swappable fans and power supplies 
- STS Listed to UL 60950 and UL 891

[View static transfer switch](https://anordmardix.com/products/static-transfer-switch/)

### UL891 switchgear

Key features:

- Designed, tested, and built to UL 891 
- Top, bottom, front, or rear cable access is possible 
- Seismically tested and certified to IBC and OSHPD

[View UL891 switchgear](https://anordmardix.com/products/ul891-switchboards/)

### UL1558 switchgear

Key features:

- Tested to ANSI C37.51 
- NEMA Type 1 enclosure design with optional segregation 
- High short time withstand up to 150kA for 1 second (60 cycles)

[View UL1558 switchgear](https://anordmardix.com/products/ul1558-switcboards/)

## Liquid cooling solutions

As AI and high-performance computing (HPC) deployments accelerate, CPUs and GPUs are becoming more powerful, causing data centers to face increased heat. Our customers are turning to [single-phase, direct-to-chip liquid cooling from JetCool](https://jetcool.com/) to cool smarter, not harder, enhancing efficiency, and sustainably boosting compute performance.

### Hybrid cooling

Key features:

- 3X denser compute
- 50kW air-cooled racks

![Hybrid cooling](https://flex.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Hybrid-Cooling-1200x798.png)

[Learn more](https://jetcool.com/smartplate-system/)

### Cold plates

Key features:

- 3X denser compute
- 1000W+ TDP support

![Cold plates](https://flex.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/SmartPlate-System-Iso-1080x-1080-e1745374935998.png)

[Learn more](https://jetcool.com/smartplate-cold-plate/)

### Direct-to-die cooling modules

Key features:

![Direct-to-die cooling module](https://flex.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/direct-to-die-cooling-e1759347394237.png)

- Remove thermal barriers and reduce thermal resistance
- Bonds directly to processor dies

[Learn more](https://jetcool.com/semiconductor-liquid-cooling/#smartlid)

- Scalable for Power-Dense Racks
