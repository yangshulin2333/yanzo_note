## Design boldly — but with less risk

Big ideas come with big unknowns. That’s why you need an experienced technology partner to help you bring your idea from prototyping to production — safely and quickly. We partner with you to vet, conceptualize, and test your product design and development.

Our advanced engineering solutions allow you to design tomorrow’s products using our expertise in [advanced manufacturing technologies](https://flex.com/solutions-and-services/manufacturing#industry-4-0), so that you can build things better, faster, and with less risk. We’re leading the revolution to transform the way things are made, and we’re always looking for ways to make our manufacturing and process technologies even faster and more efficient.

Designing new products for manufacturing requires a mix of resources and know-how across hardware and software. Partner with us for design for manufacturing (DfM) resources, design validation, and the ability to lean on our wealth of engineering experts to solve that last issue.

Team up with Flex and take advantage of our complete package of design services and product lifecycle offerings to design, source, build, deliver, and manage your products efficiently at scale.
