## The network transformation

Flex offers comprehensive services to help enable the most advanced communications solutions for 5G network infrastructure, satellite, IP, and optical networks. We partner with OEMs to manufacture critical infrastructure like radio access network (RAN), multi-access edge compute (MEC), and the telco cloud as well as emerging technologies such as open RAN.

### Flex offers a complete portfolio of solutions to deliver next-generation networks

![Communications manufacturing, design, and supply chain capabilities](https://flex.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Communications-manufacturing.jpg)

- World class design centers in Europe
- 5G radio performance testing including massive MIMO and millimeter wave
- Optical transceiver manufacturing in South East Asia
- End-to-end capabilities including customized design services, manufacturing, and integration

- Fulfillment and aftermarket services addressing the full product lifecycle including circular economy services

![What we build for the communications industry](https://flex.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/What-We-Build_500x700.jpg)

### What we deliver

- 5G RAN infrastructure
- Optical networking systems
- Optical transceivers
- Carrier routers
- High-bandwidth network switching
