## End-to-end capabilities that transform healthcare, from concept to care

As the healthcare industry rapidly evolves, Flex provides specialized end-to-end capabilities that enable medical product companies to deliver products at scale with increased quality, productivity, and speed, in every major region. With the ability to partner at any point of the product lifecycle, Flex helps healthcare companies navigate increasing complexity across the value chain to design, build, deliver, and service medical products that transform patient care.

With over 35 years of experience across [medical equipment](https://flex.com/industries/healthcare#medical-equipment), [medical devices](https://flex.com/industries/healthcare#consumer-health), and [drug delivery](https://flex.com/industries/healthcare#drug-delivery), healthcare customers turn to Flex for our user-centered design and engineering expertise, advanced manufacturing technology and vertically-integrated capabilities, and diverse supply chain network. Our comprehensive portfolio of product lifecycle capabilities is fortified by our world-class quality management system for healthcare, enabling customers to meet the most stringent compliance standards needed for Class I, II, and III medical products.

With healthcare companies redesigning their insourced operating models and collaborating with new partners to optimize operations, Flex brings the domain expertise required to accelerate innovation cycles for a range of medical products.

### Design and engineering

Leverage our patient-centered design approach, domain expertise, and deep understanding of complex product requirements across six global medical design and engineering centers. Flex’s team of [human factors](https://www.farmpd.com/expertise/human-factors-engineering) experts, design engineers provide cross-industry knowledge of a range of technologies, including connectivity, edge artificial intelligence, and sensor integration. We also specialize in multidisciplinary systems combining [optics for medical devices](https://flex.com/resources/eye-on-optics-optical-design-is-integral-in-todays-medical-products) such as imaging, illumination, and lasers.

### Advanced manufacturing

![Yellow robotic arm for advanced manufacturing](https://flex.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/Yellow-Robotic-Arm_-Flex-Juarez-2-1200x800.jpg)

Flex’s focused portfolio of advanced manufacturing technologies — including simulation, automation, robotics, digitization, and additive manufacturing — provide a competitive edge to meet your time-to-market, resiliency, and end-market requirements. Our global footprint includes 21 medical manufacturing sites across four regions and 13 countries, providing medical customers the ability to quickly adjust to changing regional, trade, and manufacturing dynamics.

With 14 FDA-certified sites and 21 sites with ISO 13485:2016 certification — the internationally recognized quality management benchmark for [medical device manufacturers](https://flex.com/industries/healthcare#consumer-health) — Flex enables customers to meet the highest quality and compliance standards for regulated medical products. Customers utilize our extensive precision [plastics](https://flex.com/solutions-and-services/manufacturing/plastics#healthcare) experience in injection molding and mold fabrication to produce and deliver the highest quality products, every time.

### Supply chain and logistics

Flex leverages our global supply chain expertise, capabilities, and digital tools to enable customers to deliver medical products to market with less complexity and risk. With a global supply chain that spans 4,000 global and local supplier relationships and 1 million parts, we help partners de-risk their complex component and inventory strategies and needs. Our vertically integrated capabilities, including advanced electronics assembly, components and materials subassembly, and systems integration, enable customers to quickly scale to high volume production.
