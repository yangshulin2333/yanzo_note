## PART 3: WIFI (Technical Layer)

### WiFi Perfected

- **Core Technologies:**
    
    - Dedicated Spectral Radio
        
    - Powerful Channel Planning
        
    - RF Environment Scanning
        
    - Flexible Authentication Methods
        
    - Centralized Remote Management
        
    - AP Analyzer and Packet Capture
        
- **Feature Deep Dive:**
    
    - **Dedicated Spectral Analyzer Radio:** Features a dedicated radio that continuously scans the environment to proactively identify interference.
        
    - **PRISM™ RF Filtering:** Active filtering blocks adjacent channel 5 GHz interference to boost SNR and preserve throughput in crowded environments.
        
    - **MLO (Multi-Link Operation):** Intelligently combines multiple Wi-Fi bands simultaneously for maximum performance and reduced latency.
        
    - **Puncturing:** WiFi 7 feature allowing higher-width channels to operate non-continuously by notching out noisy interferers.
        
    - **Coverage Visualization:** Visualize and optimize channel distribution across deployments via UniFi Site Manager.
        
    - **Precise Radio Controls:** Tuning across 2.4 GHz, 5 GHz, and 6 GHz bands; customization of channels, widths, and transmit power.
        
    - **Powerful VLAN Configurations:** Update VLAN assignments across thousands of APs instantly.
        
    - **Flexible Authentication Support:** Supports UniFi Identity, local/AD-based/cloud-hosted RADIUS, or RADIUS directly on a UniFi Gateway.
