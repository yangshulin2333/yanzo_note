### 3. OEM/ODM 解决方案 (OEM/ODM Solutions) 

**页面策略**：将“制造”升级为“联合研发与供应链服务”。明确区分 White Label (贴牌) 和 Deep Customization (深度定制)。

#### **Hero Section: The "Partnership" Hook**

- **Headline**: "Your Vision, Our Engineering. The One-Stop Station for EV Charging Manufacturing."
    
- **Sub-text**: "Accelerate your time-to-market with our turnkey engineering and manufacturing services. From concept sketches to mass production, we are your scalable backend factory."
    

#### **Section A: Cooperative Business Models (合作模式)**

_给 AI 的指令：清晰定义我们可以怎么合作，让客户对号入座。_

1. **White-Label / Private Label (快速贴牌)**
    
    - _Description_: "Choose from our existing certified product portfolio. We customize the branding, packaging, and UI language. Fastest route to market (Launch in < 4 weeks)."
        
    - _Scope_: Logo placement, CMF (Color, Material, Finish) customization, App rebranding.
        
2. **ODM Deep Customization (深度定制)**
    
    - _Description_: "We design a brand-new product from scratch based on your ID concept or technical specs. You own the IP, we handle the R&D and tooling."
        
    - _Scope_: Exclusive ID design, Custom PCBA layout, Functionality tailoring.
        
3. **SKD / CKD Assembly (散件出口)**
    
    - _Description_: "To help you reduce import tariffs and support local manufacturing mandates, we ship Semi-Knocked Down (SKD) or Completely Knocked Down (CKD) kits for local assembly."
        
    - _Benefit_: "Optimize tax efficiency and qualify for local government subsidies."
        

#### **Section B: The 5-Step Engineering Process (流程详解)**

_给 AI 的指令：这是展示专业度的核心。必须提及 DFM（面向制造的设计）和 DVP（设计验证计划）等专业术语。_

- **Step 1: Requirement & Feasibility (需求分析)**
    
    - "We analyze your target market regulations (e.g., UK Pen Fault, US UL standards) and define the Product Requirement Document (PRD)."
        
- **Step 2: ID & Structural Design (工业设计)**
    
    - "Our in-house design team creates ergonomic and aesthetic casings. We perform **DFM (Design for Manufacturing)** analysis early to ensure cost-efficiency and mass producibility."
        
- **Step 3: R&D & Prototyping (研发打样)**
    
    - _Hardware_: "Custom PCB design, component selection (using Tier-1 suppliers like Texas Instruments, STMicroelectronics)."
        
    - _Software_: "Porting OCPP stack, interface customization, and payment gateway integration."
        
    - _Output_: "Functional CNC prototypes within 15 days."
        
- **Step 4: DVP & Validation (验证测试)**
    
    - "Before mass production, we execute a rigorous **Design Validation Plan (DVP)**, including:
        
        - _Environmental_: -40°C to +80°C thermal cycling.
            
        - _Mechanical_: IK10 impact test, drop test, vibration test (simulating transportation).
            
        - _Electrical_: EMC/EMI testing, full load aging test."
            
- **Step 5: Mass Production & Logistics (量产交付)**
    
    - "Seamless transition to our ISO-certified production lines. We utilize **MES (Manufacturing Execution System)** for full traceability of every screw and chip. Global logistics support via sea, air, or rail."
        

#### **Section C: Supply Chain & Quality (供应链与质量)**

_给 AI 的指令：模仿 Jabil，强调供应链的稳定性和抗风险能力。_

- **Supply Chain Resilience**:
    
    - "We maintain strategic partnerships with global component distributors (Avnet, Arrow) to ensure priority stock allocation, mitigating global chip shortage risks."
        
- **Quality Control Standards**:
    
    - **IQC (Incoming Quality Control)**: 100% inspection of key components (Relays, LCDs, ICs).
        
    - **IPQC (In-Process Quality Control)**: Automated optical inspection (AOI) for PCBA.
        
    - **Burn-in Testing**: "Every charger undergoes a 4-8 hour full-load aging test before packaging."
        

#### **Section D: FAQ for OEM Partners (专业答疑)**

_给 AI 的指令：解决B端客户最刁钻的问题。_

- **Q: Can you help with local certification (e.g., getting UL listed in my name)?**

- **Q: Do you share my design with other competitors?**
